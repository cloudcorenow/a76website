  Our Story

About Alliance 76

A California-licensed company built on the belief that every home and business deserves world-class comfort.

1976
Est.
Founded on Principle

Built With American Pride

Alliance 76 HVAC was founded in 1976 by William "Bill" Hargrove, a Korean War veteran and master HVAC technician who believed that honest work, quality materials, and respect for the customer were the foundation of any lasting business.

What started as a one-truck operation out of a garage in Columbus, Ohio has grown into a nationally recognized HVAC firm with certified technicians throughout California, serving residential homeowners, businesses, and industrial facilities across the state.

Today, Bill's daughter Katherine leads the company as CEO, carrying on the same values her father instilled — integrity, craftsmanship, and a genuine commitment to making people comfortable in their own spaces.
